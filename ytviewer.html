Ad-Free video viewer for school

Does your school have a web filter that doesn't let you watch youtube? Then this is for you!

How to use:

1.) Look up your favorite youtube video

2.) Right below it you should see a link. at the very end of the link, there should be a bunch of random numbers and letters. copy the string right after "v="

3.) Click "new video", paste in the text, and enjoy!
